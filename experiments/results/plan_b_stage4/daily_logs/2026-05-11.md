  - pair ids,
  - image paths,
  - captions,
  - image/text/joint similarity scores,
  - bucket,
  - blank label fields.

  - `needs_audit`,

  - short annotation guideline text,
  - `stdout.log`,
  - `stderr.log`,
  - `metrics.json`,
  - `run_manifest.json`.

  `naive_union`, `joint`, and `max` together.

  files can be evaluated directly.
- Smoke scripts are now self-contained and do not share the same generated
  dataset directory, avoiding parallel-test races.
